Fried Eggs

Homepage

Description

This is the recipe for beautiful, fluffy fried eggs. A simple, quick and nutritious meal

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • 1 ts fine salt
  • 20ml Oil

Cooking Steps

  1. Heat oil in large frying pan
  2. Crack eggs open into the hot oil and sprinkle salt on top
  3. Fry until yolk hardens and white begins to brown slightly
  4. Remove from pan and allow excess oil to drip off

Best served warm on a slice of toast with some sauce. Enjoy!
