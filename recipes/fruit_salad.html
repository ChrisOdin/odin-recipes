Fruit Salad

Homepage

Description

A refreshing and sweet fruit salad. Light, juicy and full of nutrition

Ingredients

  • 100g chopped fresh banana
  • 100g fresh strawberries
  • 100g fresh chopped green apple
  • 100g fresh blackberries

Cooking Steps

  1. Add chopped banana to a bowl
  2. Add fresh strawberries to the bowl
  3. Add chopped apple to the bowl
  4. Add fresh blackberries to the bowl
  5. Mix all well together

Best served chilled with yoghurt or cream. Enjoy!
