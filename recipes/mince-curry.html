Mince Curry

Description

The following is an old faithful recipe of mine. It is a recipe for mince curry. The recipe is for a medium strength curry using beef mince along with a variety of garden vegetables and served with rice

Ingredients

  • 1 Tbs Oil
  • 1 Medium Onion
  • 1 Large Tomato
  • 400g Beef Mince
  • 2 Tbs Beef Spice
  • 2 Tbs Medium Curry Spice
  • 2 Tbs Tomato Paste
  • 500g Frozen Mixed Veg
  • 1 Cup White Rice

Steps

  1. Spice the mince
  2. Heat the oil
  3. Chop and sautee onion till brown
  4. Add mince and sautee
  5. Mix Curry powder with Tomato paste and some water
  6. Add tomato to mince and sautee for 2 min
  7. Add Curry and Paste mixture to the mince
  8. Allow to simmer for 5 min the put 400ml boiling water to pot
  9. Add mixed veg
  10. Cook for 20 min or until pot is nearly dry
  11. Cook rice
