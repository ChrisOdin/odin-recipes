Jelly Custard

Desciption

This is a very standard Jelly and Custard dish. A childhood favourite for me

Ingredients

  • 1 packet Jelly powder
  • 1 satchet Custard powder
  • 500ml Milk
  • 300ml warm Water
  • 2 Tbs Sugar

Steps

  1. Mix Water, Jelly and Sugar into a bowl
  2. Allow 30min in freezer to solidify
  3. Mix Milk, Custard and Sugar
  4. Bring to boil stiring continuously
  5. Serve cooled Custard on top of chilled Jelly
