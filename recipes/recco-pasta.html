Recco Pasta

Description

This dish is a dish inspired by my soon to be wife's favourite pasta. It is a tomato curry infused pasta with chicken or prawn pieces.

Ingredients

  • 300g chopped Chicken or Prawn meat
  • 1 Large Onion
  • 2 Large Tomatoes
  • 300g Pasta
  • 2 TBS Olive Oil
  • 1 TBS Medium Curry Powder
  • 1 Large Red Pepper

Steps

  1. Braise onion and pepper in olive oil
  2. Add Chicken or Prawn
  3. Add Curry Powder
  4. Add Tomato
  5. Add 500ml Boiled Water and allow to cook for 30 min
  6. Cook Pasta
  7. Serve
